Flux's Festival

Reject Avenue is a festival dedicated to celebrating the Flux's movement, an avant-garde art movement of the 1960s that challenged traditional boundaries between art forms and embraced the concept of art as an experiential process. The festival emphasizes the beauty of creative processes and the unpredictability inherent in design. Below is the visual identity crafted for this unique festival.
